What is Data Science?

Data Science involves using programming, statistics, and analytics to extract meaning from data. It is used across industries to make informed decisions and solve real-world problems.

Pillars of Data Science

Explore the four core pillars of data science:

  • Mathematics: Understand structures, patterns, and models
  • Statistics: Analyze trends, relationships, and probability
  • Computer Science: Automate and scale processing
  • Domain Knowledge: Apply insights to real-world fields like businesss, healthcare, or technology

Gain the skills to work with large data sets, suppory evidence-based decision making, and prepare leadership through specialized courses and work-integrated learning.
