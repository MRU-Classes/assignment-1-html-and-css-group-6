Resources

Here you'll find helpful tools, references, and support to guide your success in the MRU Data Science Minor. We understand that university-level courses in data science can be challenging - especially when juggling math, programming, and statistics. That's why we offer free tutoring, study resources, and peer support to help you stay on track. Whether you need help with assignments, want to prepare for exams, or just need someone to explain things clearly, we're here for you!
